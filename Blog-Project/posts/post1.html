Virtualization

What is?

Virtualization is an approach to pooling and sharing technology resources to simplify management and increase asset use so that IT resources can more readily meet business demand.

With servers or networks, virtualization is used to take a single physical asset and make it operate as if it were multiple assets. This improves asset utilization and efficiency, and decreases costs by reducing the need for physical assets.
